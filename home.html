The Distillery

Since the early 1800’s The Distillery has served as Manila’s neighborhood convenience store. Located in the heart of Mandaluyong, we proudly serve a great collection of Liquor, Wine, and much more to our community. We stock and support local brands as well as international brands, and have everything from hard to find Beer to Whiskey and much more liquors.
